Learn About the Different Types of Laser Wrinkles Treatment

To understand what type of treatment is best for you, it’s important to know about the different types of wrinkles. There are three main types of wrinkles: those caused by gravity, those caused by sun exposure, and those caused by aging. Gravity wrinkles occur when the skin becomes stretched or pulled too much from being overweight or too weightless; sun exposure wrinkles happen when the skin spends time in direct sunlight; and aging wrinkles happen as a result of changes in DNA that occur over time.
